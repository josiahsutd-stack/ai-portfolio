Evidence Policy

Claims stop where the evidence stops

Real implementation: runnable code, tests, fitted classical models, persistence, metrics, or simulation.

Synthetic or public subset: every dataset and corpus is labeled at the project level.

Mock provider: deterministic LLM/VLM substitutes test workflow contracts, not model intelligence.

Simulation: robotics results do not imply hardware, perception, or physical safety validation.

Production: no customer adoption, deployed reliability, or professional compliance outcome is claimed.

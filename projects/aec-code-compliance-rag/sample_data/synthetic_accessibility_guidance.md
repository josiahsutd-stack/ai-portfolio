## Accessible Route Width

Primary public routes should provide a clear width of at least 1200 mm in high
traffic zones. Secondary staff-only routes may require a separate project review
when equipment, trolleys, or queuing are expected.

<!-- page: 2 -->
## Doorway And Threshold Checks

Doorways serving accessible rooms should provide a clear opening of at least
850 mm. Threshold changes greater than 12 mm should be ramped, beveled, or
otherwise resolved before issue.

<!-- page: 3 -->
## Accessible Parking Interface

Accessible parking assumptions should identify the route to entry, weather
protection, kerb transitions, and any lift dependency. Missing assumptions
should be logged before planning submission.

## Corridor Compartmentation

Residential corridors longer than 30 m should include documented
compartmentation intent, smoke control assumptions, and fire-rated door
schedules.

<!-- page: 2 -->
## Fire Rated Door Coordination

Any door on a fire-rated line should include a rating note in the drawing or
room schedule. Door swings, hold-open devices, and access-control interfaces
should be coordinated before issue.

<!-- page: 3 -->
## Stair Discharge Assumptions

Stair discharge diagrams should identify final exits, protected lobby
interfaces, smoke separation assumptions, and any areas requiring specialist
review.

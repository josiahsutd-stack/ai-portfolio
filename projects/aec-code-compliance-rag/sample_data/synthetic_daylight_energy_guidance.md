## Daylight And Glazing Review

Frequently occupied rooms should receive useful daylight where feasible. South
and west glazing should be reviewed for solar gain, glare, and cooling impact.

<!-- page: 2 -->
## Deep Floor Plate Mitigation

Deep floor plates should include a daylight mitigation strategy such as atria,
light wells, borrowed light, or program zoning.

<!-- page: 3 -->
## Envelope Assumption Log

Envelope submissions should record glazing ratio, shading intent, thermal
bridge assumptions, and any unresolved facade-performance risks.

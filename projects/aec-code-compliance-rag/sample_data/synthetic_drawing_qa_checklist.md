## Room Schedule QA

Every issued room schedule should include a unique room ID, room name, modeled
area, scheduled area, finish material, door clearance, accessibility note where
relevant, and unresolved coordination comments.

<!-- page: 2 -->
## Drawing Issue Completeness

Drawing issue notes should identify revision, discipline owner, outstanding
coordination items, linked model status, and any assumptions carried into the
next review cycle.

<!-- page: 3 -->
## Model And Sheet Consistency

Model-derived schedules should be checked against sheet annotations for door
tags, room names, finish codes, fire-rating notes, and accessibility comments.

# Limitations

- The only evaluation input is one authored synthetic scenario. It demonstrates contract behavior, not external validity.
- Requirement extraction uses a deterministic phrase grammar. It does not robustly interpret unrestricted project conversation.
- Roles are strings in local fixtures; there is no identity, authentication, authorization service, or signed approval record.
- Site dimensions, setbacks, limits, access points, wind direction, and objective weights are supplied values. The workflow does not infer or verify them against surveys, planning controls, or building codes.
- Massing geometry uses rectangular proxy models. Environmental scores are transparent heuristics rather than daylight, energy, wind, structural, traffic, or life-safety simulations.
- The selected mass footprints are converted into room-like rectangles only to exercise a typed handoff. They are not functional spaces or a developed plan.
- The takeoff covers one schematic ground-floor envelope. It omits most building systems, temporary works, preliminaries, design development, procurement, escalation, taxes, risk, and professional measurement rules.
- Rates are synthetic and explicitly not market prices. The cost output is unsuitable for budgeting, procurement, valuation, or commercial decisions.
- Budget comparison is disabled because the approved project budget and schematic-envelope cost have incompatible scopes.
- Tender analysis is not run because no tender is generated or supplied for the derived plan.
- No professional, authority, customer, site, or construction validation has been performed.
